    My Waterpolo icons

    U_manzi56 October 22, 2025

    1. Filip Filipović

    Filip Filipović of Serbia is widely considered one of the greatest players in the history of the sport. He won gold medals with the Serbian national team at the Olympics (2016, 2020) and multiple world and European championships. Wikipedia His ability to score from difficult positions and his longevity at the highest level set him apart.

    2. Ioannis Fountoulis

    Ioannis (Giannis) Fountoulis from Greece is another top‑tier talent. He’s been a prolific scorer for both club and country, serving as captain of the Greek national team and earning major honours in international tournaments. Wikipedia His leadership and scoring power make him a key figure in modern water polo.

    3. Andrija Prlainović

    Andrija Prlainović of Serbia has also built a storied career with numerous international titles. He was part of the Serbian teams that won multiple world championships and Olympic gold medals. Wikipedia His consistency across years and tournaments makes him one of the elite.

    4. Tamás Molnár

     

    From Hungary, Tamás Molnár is a former great who earned three Olympic gold medals (2000, 2004, 2008) with the Hungarian national team. Wikipedia His achievements place him among the legends of the sport.
